About FlightME

Your AI-powered assistant for smarter flight booking decisions

Our Mission

FlightME combines web scraping technology with advanced AI language models to provide travelers with real-time insights on flight pricing trends and the optimal times to book flights.

We aim to demystify the complex world of flight pricing by leveraging data analysis and AI to help you save money and make informed booking decisions.

How It Works

  1. Data Collection

    We scrape flight pricing data from multiple airline websites and booking platforms.

  2. Data Processing

    The collected data is cleaned, structured, and prepared for analysis.

  3. AI Analysis

    Our AI analyzes the data to identify trends and generate price predictions.

  4. Insight Generation

    You receive personalized insights and recommendations for your specific travel plans.

Key Features

Fare Trend Analysis

Analyze historical pricing data to identify trends and predict future fare fluctuations.

Optimal Booking Times

Receive recommendations on the best times to book flights for the lowest fares.

Real-Time Insights

Get up-to-date information based on the latest flight pricing data.

Technology Behind FlightME

Python Backend

Powered by Flask for a robust and efficient server-side application.

Web Scraping

Using Selenium to gather real-time flight pricing data from multiple sources.

AI Integration

Leveraging OpenAI's GPT models for sophisticated price analysis and predictions.

Data Visualization

Creating intuitive charts and graphs to help you understand pricing trends.
